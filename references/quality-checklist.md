# Quality Checklist

Before finalizing a PRD, check:

- Is the one-sentence summary concrete?
- Are target users explicit?
- Is the business goal separated from user value?
- Is the current flow or current problem clear?
- Are scope in and out explicit?
- Are key modules described with rules, not just nouns?
- Are error and edge cases described?
- Is there an implementation-thinking section when needed?
- Are assumptions and open questions visible?
- Are diagrams added where they reduce ambiguity?
- Could engineering start without guessing the main behavior?
